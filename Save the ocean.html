Save The Ocean

Learn about SDG 14: Life Below Water

Why Are Oceans Important?

Oceans cover more than 70% of our planet. They provide food, oxygen and regulate our climate!

Fun Facts About the Ocean

Did you know that Oceans produce over half of the worlds oxygen?

Did you know that Coral reefs are home to more than 25% of marine life?

Did you know that plastic pollution is one of the biggest threats to marine life?

How Can We Help?

  1. Reduce, reuse and recycle plastic to stop ocean pollution.

  2. Support marine protected areas to protect ocean wildlife.

  3. Join a beach lineup or start one in your community.

Let's work together to protect our oceans!
